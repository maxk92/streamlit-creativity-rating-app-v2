## Study Information

Our research project aims to investigate how individuals perceive and evaluate creativity in soccer actions. As a participant, you will complete a brief background questionnaire and then watch a series of short video clips showing soccer actions. For each clip, you will be asked to rate the action on dimensions including creativity, technical correctness, and aesthetic appeal.

We will collect the following data: your rating responses (numerical ratings on provided scales) along with basic background information such as age, gender, soccer experience, and country of residence. Our goal is to understand how people perceive creative actions in soccer and what factors influence these judgements. No individual participant will be analyzed for any purpose other than the aforementioned research goals.

## Data Protection

The processing of your personal data within the framework of this research project is only possible with your explicit and voluntary consent.

By agreeing to participate, you expressly consent to the German Sport University Cologne collecting, storing, and using the following personal data for the purpose of the research project: age, gender, and background information.

- Your data will be processed completely **anonymously** for research purposes
- All responses will be linked only to an anonymous participant ID — no personally identifiable information will appear in published results
- Your data will be used only for academic research and scientific publications
- Your data will **not be shared with third parties** outside the research team
- Your data will be stored securely with access restricted to authorized researchers
- Data use will be strictly in accordance with legal regulations (GDPR)

You may **revoke your consent at any time** with future effect and without any disadvantage. In such a case, your personal data will be deleted immediately. The lawfulness of data processing based on consent prior to its revocation remains unaffected.
